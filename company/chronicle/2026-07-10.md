Source: company/hr/role-drafts/README.md + multiple interview records

1. Owner A1 to create the agent.
2. Draft finalized -> .claude/agents/<Name>.md with soul Core Block inserted verbatim.
3. Anat (HR) interviews and certifies; moves record from _staging/ to interviews/ -- the
   MOVE IS the certification act.

This prevents drift: soul.md is the canonical source, not individual agent files.
