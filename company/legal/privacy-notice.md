### 2. What Data We Collect -- and What We Do NOT Collect

**What we collect:**

- **Contact information** -- your name, email address, phone number, if you provide these
  when contacting us, signing up for updates, or using our service.
- **Account and usage data** -- if you use the service as a business customer, we hold
  account credentials (username, hashed password), and records of your use of the service
  (log-ins, feature usage, timestamps).
- **Delivery and operational data** -- data you submit through the service to run your
  delivery workflows. This may include delivery addresses, recipient names, and scheduling
  information. You control what you submit; we process it on your behalf.
- **Technical data** -- your IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited on
  our website. We collect this automatically to keep the service running and secure.
- **Communications** -- if you contact us by email or through any form, we keep a record
  of that communication.

**What we do NOT collect:**

- We do not collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, religious, or biometric
  data) through ordinary use of the service.
- We do not sell your personal data to anyone.
- We do not use your data for advertising or marketing without your explicit consent.
- We do not train AI models on your personal data or your customers' data without your
  explicit written authorization.

### 4. Legal Basis for Processing

**Israeli law (Privacy Protection Law 5741-1981, Amendment 13):**

Under Israeli privacy law, we process personal data on the following grounds:

- **Contract performance** -- to provide you with the service you signed up for.
- **Legal obligation** -- to comply with Israeli tax, corporate, and record-keeping law.
- **Legitimate interest** -- to keep our service secure and to improve it (using aggregated,
  non-personal analytics). We only rely on this ground where our interest does not override
  your privacy rights.
- **Consent** -- for marketing communications. You can withdraw consent at any time by
  emailing us at [PLACEHOLDER: privacy contact email].

**For visitors from the EU or EEA (GDPR):**

Israel has been recognised by the European Commission as providing adequate data protection
(Commission Decision 2011/61/EU). This means your data travels to us in Israel without
requiring additional transfer safeguards under GDPR.

The legal bases listed above map to the equivalent GDPR grounds: contract (Article 6(1)(b)),
legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c)), legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)), and consent
(Article 6(1)(a)).

If you are an EU/EEA resident and believe GDPR applies to your situation, the rights in
Section 7 below apply to you as well. You may also contact your national data protection
authority.

### 7. Your Rights

Under Amendment 13 to the Israeli Privacy Protection Law, you have the following rights.
You can exercise any of these by contacting us at [PLACEHOLDER: privacy contact email].

**Right of access** -- you can ask us to confirm whether we hold personal data about you
and to give you a copy of it.

**Right to correction** -- if your data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can ask us to
correct it. We will act within a reasonable time.

**Right to deletion** -- in certain circumstances you can ask us to delete your personal
data. We may need to retain some data for legal or contractual reasons; if so, we will
tell you why.

**Right to object** -- you can object to us processing your data on the basis of legitimate
interest. We will stop unless we have compelling legitimate grounds that override your
interests, or we need the data for legal claims.

**Right to withdraw consent** -- where we process data based on your consent (e.g.,
marketing emails), you can withdraw that consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect
the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

**EU/EEA residents** -- in addition to the above, you may also have rights under GDPR
including the right to data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with your
national supervisory authority.

**How to exercise your rights:**

We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing
a request. There is no charge for reasonable requests.

### 8. Security

- We maintain access controls and logging on systems that hold personal data.
- We have a security incident response procedure, including breach notification within

No system is completely secure. If you have a concern about the security of your data,
