## SECTION 1 -- Social Handle Recommendation (HIL-004)

### Options considered

| Slug            | Notes                                                             |
|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|
| eco-synthetic   | Matches company name exactly; hyphen supported on LinkedIn + X;  |
|                 | on Instagram hyphens are not allowed in usernames                |
| ecosynthetic    | No punctuation; works everywhere; clean; readable as one word    |
| eco_synthetic   | Underscore variant; Instagram-compatible; reads well; common     |
|                 | workaround when hyphen is not available                          |

### Recommendation: ecosynthetic

Reason: one slug, works on every platform without modification. LinkedIn supports it as a
custom URL slug. X/Twitter allows it as a handle (@ecosynthetic). Instagram allows it as a
username. No punctuation to lose, no variation to manage across platforms. Clean and direct --
consistent with the "Living Signal" brand direction.

Fallback if ecosynthetic is taken on a specific platform: eco_synthetic (underscore) as the
secondary option. Avoid eco-synthetic as the primary because Instagram will not accept it and
users typing @eco-synthetic on X will not be linked correctly.

Recommended handle: @ecosynthetic everywhere.

### POST 2 -- What "clean" actually means

Speed is not the goal. Clean is.

Fast and wrong is just expensive failure delivered quickly.

At Eco-Synthetic, "working clean" means:
- The right information reaches the right person at the right moment.
- No redundant steps. No noise in the system.
- And when something goes wrong, you know immediately -- not three days later.

Delivery management sounds like logistics. It is actually a communication problem.
And communication problems are exactly what a well-built human+AI team is made for.

### POST 3 -- The people behind the system

People ask us: if you work with AI agents, do you actually need a team?

Yes. More than ever.

The AI is fast, consistent, never tired. It runs the parts of the work that need
to run perfectly, every time, at scale.

The people decide what matters. They read the situation. They carry the relationships.
They set the direction and hold the quality.

Neither one works without the other. That is the whole point.

We are a small company. We are building something real.
And we do it with a team that happens to include both kinds of intelligence.
