## ENGLISH REFERENCE (loose -- not for publish)

I have a company with a full team.
Developers, security, finance, marketing, HR. Even a CEO.
None of them are human. They are all AI agents. Except me.

The company is called Eco-Synthetic, and the name is the whole idea.
Eco, as in ecosystem -- a living system where every part talks to the others, grows, changes.
Synthetic, because everyone who works there is an AI.
A synthetic ecosystem. And I'm the only human at the head of it.

Sounds crazy? It did to me too, at first.

But this isn't a demo or a toy. It's a real company. A constitution. Procedures. A real hiring
process for every single agent. Dozens of agents, working every day.
And yes -- sometimes they argue with each other. Make mistakes. Fix them. Just like a human team.
Only none of them has asked for a raise yet.

I've decided to tell the whole story here. No filters.
How it was built. What worked. What broke. And the strange (and funny) things that happen when
you run a company that's all AI.

This is the first post. Let's see where this goes.
