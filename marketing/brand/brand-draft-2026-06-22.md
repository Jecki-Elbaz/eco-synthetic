### MERGED DIRECTION -- "Living Signal"

**Concept**

Eco-Synthetic is a living system: it breathes, adapts, and grows the way an ecosystem does --
through relationships, not commands. But it executes like a precision instrument. Clean lines,
no noise, no wasted motion. The brand holds both truths at once: organic in its values, exact
in its craft. When you look at it, you feel that something intelligent is at work, and that a
person is behind it.

This is not tech-cold. It is not nature-soft. It is the third thing: a system that has
both. The warmth shows up in the materials -- earth, forest, warmth of skin-tone off-white.
The precision shows up in how everything is used: sparingly, with intention, never decorated
for its own sake.

**Color palette**

| Name           | Hex     | Role                                                            |
|----------------|---------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Forest Ink     | #1B3A2D | Primary dark anchor (from C); deep authority, organic roots   |
| Sage Mist      | #7EAB55 | Secondary organic (from A); growth, motion, breath            |
| Warm Cream     | #F5F0E8 | Background (from A); warmth that keeps it from going cold     |
| Carbon Precise | #1C1C1E | Dark text and structure (from A); the engineering side        |
| Mint Precise   | #4ECBA1 | Action/accent (from C); clarity, forward motion, digital clean|

Palette rationale: Forest Ink anchors the dark with more organic depth than Carbon alone.
Sage Mist keeps the living-ecosystem warmth as secondary organic. Warm Cream backgrounds hold
the human touch. Mint Precise as the action color gives the clean signal pop that C was known
for, without the Rust accent (removed -- too dissonant against the green family). Carbon
Precise handles structure text.

Palette feel: a forest in the morning, seen through a precision instrument. Still alive,
perfectly clear.

**Typography pairing**

- Heading: Cormorant Garamond (Google Fonts) -- from Option A. Editorial serif with organic
  curves. Reads as crafted, not manufactured. The "owned by people" signal. At large display
  sizes it brings the life that Option A promised.
- Body: Source Sans 3 (Google Fonts) -- from Option C. Open, readable, warm enough to carry
  the human touch quality. Clean, no noise, zero ambiguity at small sizes. Better fit for the
  merged direction than DM Sans -- slightly warmer, still precise.

Pairing rationale: Cormorant brings the living quality; Source Sans 3 delivers the clean signal.
The contrast between them is the brand in miniature: organic heading, precise body.

**One-line concept statement**

Nature's intelligence, engineering's discipline -- a living system that runs clean.
