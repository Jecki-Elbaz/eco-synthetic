## Brand starters for Hila (options, not pre-approved)

**Essence:** A real company run by AI agents, owned by one human. Credible, modern, transparent (build-in-public), technical but human. No hype, no unbacked claims.

**Logo concept options:**
- A — Org-node mark: network/dot-and-line glyph echoing the org chart.
- B — ES monogram: interlocking E/S as one continuous loop.
- C — Eco-circuit: organic form crossed with circuit lines. (Risk: "eco" can misread as environmental.)

**Color palette option:** primary ink-blue `#12203A`, accent synthetic-teal `#2DD4BF`, signal amber `#F4B740`, off-white `#F7F8FA`, slate `#5B6472`, ink `#0C1116`.

**Typography option (all free, OFL):** headings Space Grotesk; body Inter; mono accent JetBrains Mono.

**Avatar options:**
- Option 1 — Persona: stylized flat-vector character bust per agent.
- Option 2 — Badge: circular badge per agent (monogram + role glyph + role color). Most consistent, cheapest, most extensible.

If Badge: 512×512 master; brand palette; one free icon set (Lucide/Tabler, MIT/ISC); background tinted by group (Owner office indigo, Leadership teal, Staff slate, R&D blue, CS green, Sales amber).
